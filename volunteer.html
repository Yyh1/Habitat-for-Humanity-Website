    Sign up

    Information for Individual Volunteers

    How to Sign Up:

    Please email us at habitat@rice.edu, with your name, the dates you would like to volunteer, and whether or not you will be able to drive others (you have a car).

    When and Where:

    Unless the site leader says otherwise, we will always meet at 7 AM at the Rice Sallyport (near the north colleges). A small bagel breakfast will be provided and we should leave by 7:15 AM. We will arrive back on campus sometime between 2 & 3 PM.

    What to Bring:

    Working clothes and CLOSED TOED shoes- there is a high chance these clothes will get dirty!
    Light jacket - mornings are cold sometimes.
    NO need for Lunch - The site leader will buy lunch.
    Water bottles.
    Waiver- If you do not bring a filled out and signed waiver, know your Health Insurance Carrier & Policy Number for the waiver you must fill out and sign in the morning.

    Information for Group Builds

    Going on a habitat for humanity build is a great team-building opportunity, where you will benefit from working on a project together as a group and at the same time helping out a family in need, all while having a great time!
